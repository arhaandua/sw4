Achievements

Art
My school wins prizes in Art competitions

We are so fortunate to have a Visual Arts program at Del Rey School. Students begin to visit the art studio in first grade and continue through the fifth grade. The art curriculum is sequential so that students build artistic skills and familiarity with materials and art genres as they progress through the grades. We focus on the creative process and more importantly seek to impart a love of the arts!

Physical Education
My school wins prizes in Physical Education competitions

Fun for Everyone is our motto for physical education “PE” at Del Rey. Student’s success, joy in activity, sportsmanship and inclusivity are paramount. Lessons include physical fitness “Fitness First,” skill development and lively games.

Innovation
My school wins prizes in Innovation competitions

Our school now boasts a room for creating and innovating! All grade levels engage students through project based learning, and we have a lab space for creative projects.
