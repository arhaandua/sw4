what is new

  • CULTIVATING ETHICAL AND RESPECTFUL CITIZENS OUSD believes strongly in cultivating ethical and respectful citizens; kids who have great character. Currently, we use programs like KidPower, Think First, Speak Up, Be Safe and Epoch to help students, teachers, parents and staff promote a culture that supports strong character.

    CULTIVATING ETHICAL AND RESPECTFUL CITIZENS OUSD believes strongly in cultivating ethical and respectful citizens; kids who have great character. Currently, we use programs like KidPower, Think First, Speak Up, Be Safe and Epoch to help students, teachers, parents and staff promote a culture that supports strong character.

  • SERVICE PROJECTS Through our student council, we participate in various service projects, some of which include canned food drives and food distribution, warm coats, shoes, pajamas for the crisis center for children, book drives, and more. We hope to provide students with a chance to give back to their community by serving others.
